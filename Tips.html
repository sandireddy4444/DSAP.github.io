Water Conservation Tips

  • Aim for shorter showers and consider turning off the water while soaping

  • Set up a rainwater harvesting system to collect rainwater for outdoor use, such as watering plants and gardens.

  • Instead of using a hose to clean driveways and sidewalks, use a broom to sweep away debris.

  • Insulate hot water pipes to reduce the time it takes for hot water to reach the faucet, saving both water and energy.

  • Consider upgrading to water-efficient appliances like dishwashers and washing machines that use less water per cycle.
